Irimo Dushaka Icyo Wifuza

Kugirango werekane ibisobanuro by'ifashayobora muri iri dirishya, kanda umutwe runaka mu mwanya Wibirimo. kanda akarango mpandeshatu iruhande rw'umutwe maze urebe ibigize.

Kugirango ubone umutwe w'ifashayobora ka andika ijambo cyangwa interuro mur mwanya w'ishakishamaze ubone iyo mitwe. Kanda kumutwe umwe maze usome ibijyanye nawo. niba iryo shakisha ryawe ntacyo rikuzniye, gerageze wandike aagambo make cyangwa ugende ukomatanya amagambo mu buryo butanduanye. ibisubizo biragenda biza mu mwanya w'ishakisha iruhande uo ugendawandika. Kudirango ubone ibiri mu mwanyaruhande, kanda kuri buto yo gufunga mu mwanyaruhande w'ishakisha, siba umwanya w'ishakisha cyangwa ukande kuri, Esc.

Kugirango ubone ibijyanye n'umutwe w'ifashayobora uri gusoma, kanda ku mahuza ari mu idirishya ry'ifashayobora nko mu mbuga zisanzwe.ushobora kadi gusubira inyuma cyangwa imbere ku mapaji wigeze gusura ukoresheje uto buto yo kGusubira imnyma n'iyo kujya Imbere.

Inama y'Ishakisha

Niba utabonye icyo washakaga dore ibishobora kugufasha:

  • Umenye ko uri gushaka ikintu kijyanye na &brandShortName; iri si ishakisha risanzwerya interineti.
  • Gerageza kwagura ishakisha ryawe; nturihine cyane. amagambo yawe agomba gutandukana n'ay'ishakisha.
  • Wirinde kwagura cyane ishakisha ryawe. urugero, ijambo rusange nka "page" rizazana amakuru menshi kurusha ayo ukeneye.
